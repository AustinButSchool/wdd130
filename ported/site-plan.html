Overview

Purpose

The purpose of Ported is to create a place for people to be able to see what projects their peers are working on, as well as post updates about their projects. This will be an easily accsesable space to have people see what their peers are working on.

Audience

The audience for this website would be for peers and friends to have a secure place to share off their personal projects and ideas to others. It would be for people who would want a smaller community to show off their projects, as well as a spot to share updates.
